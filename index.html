Demo Submission Guidelines

Submission Criteria:

  1. Audio Format: Please submit your demo in WAV format, ensuring a high-quality sound experience. We require 24-bit integer audio files to capture the richness and depth of your music.
  2. Finished Songs Only: Send us your best work! We're seeking completed, polished tracks that showcase your full artistic vision. No works in progress, please. Let your finished composition speak volumes about your unique sound.
  3. Original Compositions: We thrive on originality. While we appreciate the art of remixing, for demo submissions, we're focused on your original compositions. Share the music that represents the true essence of your creativity.

Submission Process:

  1. Prepare Your Demo: Ensure your song is in WAV format, 24-bit integer, and is a finished, standalone piece.
  2. Attach it to the Email: Include your WAV file, along with any additional information you'd like us to know, such as a brief artist bio or the inspiration behind the track. Attach all of it to the Email or if its file size exceeds the limit, upload it to Google Drive or similar file hosting platforms and send us the link. Make sure we have access to the files.
  3. Submit Your Demo: Send your ZIP folder to demo@polarisrecords.com with the subject line "Demo Submission: [Your Artist Name]." Feel free to include links to your social media profiles or website if you wish.
  4. Await Our Response: Our A&R team will carefully review each submission. We appreciate your patience as we curate our constellation of artists. If your demo resonates with us, you'll receive a response within a reasonable timeframe.
